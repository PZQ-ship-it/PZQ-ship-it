Selected Research 科研经历

CogPace-Bench: A Benchmark for Cognitive Pacing and Graph-based Reasoning in LLM Educational Agents

First Author | Advisor: Haobo Wang

Feb 2026 — Present

  • Research Overview: Developed the first dynamic benchmark combining knowledge graph distance and cognitively-restricted virtual student agents to address the expert blind spot and cognitive overload in LLM tutoring.
  • Graph-based Reasoning Path Tracing: Built a dynamic sandbox driven by local KGs to transcend static evaluation. Engineered an interceptor engine to map entities to KGs, and proposed Graph Semantic Jump Distance (GSJD) metric to capture far-jump logical gaps, quantifying the coherence of multi-step pedagogical reasoning.
  • Dynamic Adaptation and Pedagogical Stealth Quantification: Simulated student cognitive overload to evaluate dynamic pacing and epistemic humility. Introduced Mechanical Awareness Penalty (MAP) via LLM-as-a-judge to detect the Uncanny Valley effect, measuring pedagogical stealth and learning flow maintenance.
  • Project Outcomes: Formulated two novel evaluation metrics (GSJD and MAP) and constructed a complete dynamic sandbox evaluation framework, establishing a robust foundation for an upcoming academic publication or open-source release.
  • Code: https://github.com/PZQ-ship-it/CogPace-Bench

Digital Textbook: Core Algorithm Research for End-to-End Intelligent Authoring Platforms

Core Algorithm Researcher | Advisor: Haobo Wang

Dec 2025 — Present

  • Research Overview: Constructed a full-link generation and review architecture integrating GraphRAG and neuro-symbolic engines to address logical drift and attention decay in long-form professional textbook creation.
  • Multi-hop Reasoning Generation via GraphRAG: Built a heterogeneous KG using Milvus and implemented BFS topology-aware recall to inject long-term memory, improving cross-chapter logical consistency by 38.4%.
  • Dynamic Context Pruning and Multi-dimensional Alignment Control: Established a dual-layer pipeline and proposed a context pruning mechanism based on graph topological distance to eliminate noise for improved outline alignment. Achieved a 99% violation interception rate and increased the overall error detection rate to 71.4%, while reducing inference costs by 35%.
  • Project Outcomes: Successfully implemented a fully automated pipeline for professional textbook authoring and reviewing. The system effectively resolved critical challenges in long-form generation, ensuring rigorous cross-chapter logical consistency, maintaining intra-chapter semantic coherence, and achieving theme-adaptive stylistic alignment.

GaokaoLLM: High-Performance Agentic RAG Decision Support System

Team Leader & Core Model Researcher | Advisor: Haobo Wang

Jan 2025 — June 2025

  • Research Overview: Researched a robust Agentic RAG architecture to address knowledge hallucination and long reasoning chains in high-stakes educational decision-making.
  • System Architecture and Consistency Verification: Architected a state-driven engine incorporating multi-stage intent routing and dynamic rewriting nodes; implemented a consistency verification mechanism to enforce key-field constraints, achieving 97.8% risk interception accuracy.
  • Reasoning Acceleration and Optimization: Applied Chain of Draft (CoD) prompting to high-frequency nodes, eliminating redundant reasoning and reducing Time to First Token (TTFT) by 43%.
  • Retrieval Engine Optimization: Developed a BGE-M3 based hybrid search engine with BCEmbedding reranking; achieved 95.3% Top-5 recall for long-tail matching with P99 latency under 400ms.
  • Project Outcomes: Designed and deployed a dynamic conversational routing engine capable of real-time intent recognition and entity extraction. The system successfully manages the distribution and specific retrieval for 5 distinct query categories (e.g., major recommendations, score inquiries, course requirements), while dynamically constructing user profiles during interactions to ensure highly precise and personalized decision support.
  • Code: https://github.com/PZQ-ship-it/gaokaollmmodel
